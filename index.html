I'm an 18 year old whose interest in programming began in Secondary 3 when I first took O-Level Computing. This early exposure sparked a curiosity that led me to explore various areas of computer science, including competitive programming through the National Olympiad in Informatics (NOI). Beyond academics, I serve as the Secretary of the Anglo-Chinese Junior College 49th Students’ Council, where I help lead school-wide initiatives and continue to hone my leadership, communication, and organisational abilities.
